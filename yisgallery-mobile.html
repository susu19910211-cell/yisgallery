About YIS Gallery

ABOUT US

YIS believes that art should not exist solely within white walls, but flow into the rhythms of contemporary living.

YIS Gallery 创立于2025年，是致力于当代艺术展览策划、艺术家推广、艺文活动运营的独立艺术空间。
